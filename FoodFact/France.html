Ah France, my country. I'm not proud of it in every way, but when it comes to food, I shout "cocorico". Anyway, enough talk, let's move on to the selection of my 3 favourite French dishes. Bon appétit.

1. The Savoyard fondue

Fondue Savoyarde is a regional dish of Savoy gastronomy made with melted cheese and bread (=probably the two things that I love the most in life). It is prepared using the so-called half and half method, using local products such as Comté (=best cheese of all time), Beaufort, or Gruyère. Here is a wonderful recipe of a Savoyard fondue!

2. Gratin dauphinois

Gratin dauphinois is a traditional French dish made of potatoes and milk. As one of the few French dishes without cheese that I love, it deserves its place on my site. Why don't you try it yourself with a little help from this page ?

3. The galette des rois

To the memory of those moments spent in family around a good galette des rois, or a good king's cake. Its frangipane pastry, with a slightly almondy taste, is what makes it so special. If you've never eaten it, you need to do something about it! Discover how it taste thanks to this recipe.
