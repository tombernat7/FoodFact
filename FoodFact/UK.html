1. Full english breakfast

If I want to respect the vision of the English food that I have, I have to mention the full english breakfast. This is unsual to me, and yet very welcome. An exemple of a recipe can be found here .

2. Fish and chips

Still through this very singular vision of British cuisine, let's continue to explore this universe with a meal that has become world famous : fish and chips. Some fried fish, some chips, and sometimes some sauce to dry it all out. Perfect for takeaway ! Easy to make by yourself, and you will find some guide lines here .
