2. Lassi

Not something we eat for once ! Let's talk about lassi. Basically, it's a simple yogurt-based beverage. And by simple, I mean really simple. It consists of just yogurt, water, and typically some spices and/or fruit. For me, there is only one lassi, and it's the mango lassi. And on top of this, this is easy to make, proof here .
